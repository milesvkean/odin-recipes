Mushroom Quesadillas

Description:

Yum quesadillas. I like to add mushrooms and serve it with guacamole. It reminds me of when I was a bell hop at a hotel and it was one of the employee meals the restaurant served. Always my favorite.

Ingredients

  • flour tortillas
  • chihuahua cheese
  • mushrooms sliced thin
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Slice mushrooms thin and cook on medium-low heat with olive oil until fully cooked.
  2. Place cheese and cooked mushrooms on one half of the flour tortilla, fold it in half.
  3. Cook on medium heat until both sides of tortilla are turning brown and cheese melts.
  4. Cut in thirds and serve with homemade guac or salsa.
