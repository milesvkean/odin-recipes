Chile Serrano Salsa Recipe

Description:

I was searching for salsa recipes online and none of them seemed quite right so I asked my friend from Mexico how she makes it. Without hesitation she sent me the following recipe and I love it. It's nice and spicy. Very healthy and addictive.

Ingredients

  • 7 tomatoes
  • 3 serrano peppers
  • 1 large clove garlic
  • cilantro
  • salt
  • lime
  • 1/4 of an onion

Steps:

  1. Place the tomatoes, serrano peppers, and garlic clove in a pot of water and bring to a boil until the tomato skins start to peel
  2. Place all items in a blender
