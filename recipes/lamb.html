Two Hour Lamb With Red Wine Olive Oil & Fresh Herbs

Description:

I learned this from my Serbian roommate in New York. He made it one time for a religious holiday and it was amazing. Here is the recipe:

Ingredients

  • Lamb
  • Bottle of red wine (we recommend Malbec from Argentina or Sangiovese)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • Oregano

Steps:

  1. Turn on oven to 300F
  2. Add all the ingredients to the lamb
  3. Cover and put in oven for two hours
  4. Serve with side of vegetables and glass of wine
