Claire's Stuffing

Description:

My little sister asked if I had any requests for Thanksgiving where she usually cooks mostly African food. I said stuffing. She apparently had never made it before but it turned out incredibly good, the best I've ever had. And here is her recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of butter
  • One whole diced onion
  • Two diced stalks of celery
  • 9 cups of dried bread cubes (you can buy that at walmart)
  • Seasoning: sage, italian herbs, garlic powder, natures seasoning, black pepper
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of beef broth

Steps:

  1. Heat 2 sticks of butter on stove.
  2. Add one whole diced onion and two diced stalks of celery.
  3. Heat for 5-10min on medium.
  4. In large baking pan add 9 cups of dried bread cubes.
  5. Season with sage, italian herbs, garlic powder, natures seasoning, black pepper.
  6. Add 1 cup of chicken broth and 2 cups of beef broth.
  7. Pour butter and onion celery mixture over and stir together.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for an hour on 350.
